Ramen

Description

Slow cooked pork shoulder, with pork bone based broth, chopped green onions, a seasoned egg cut in half, and hand pulled noodles.

ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Pork bone broth
  • Green onion
  • Seasoned egg
  • Noodles

  2. Prepare the broth
  3. Chop the green onions
  4. Cut the egg
  5. Cook the noodles
  6. Combine everything in a bowl
